Components (scarlet.component)¶

The base unit of scarlet, Component requires an initial guess of the center, SED, and morphology of the objects in a scene. Multiple components can be grouped together as a ComponentTree with additional constraints being put on the centers, SEDs, and morphologies of all components in the tree. A ComponentTree is hierarchical, so its components can be both Component and other ComponentTree instances.
